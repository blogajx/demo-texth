Privacy Policy

Transparency about how we handle your data.

Effective Date: January 2026

At RememberingHadi.online (maintained by NexGuard Web Solutions), we value your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in data minimization. As a memorial website, we do not require users to create accounts. However, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Voluntary Information: Name and email address if you choose to contact us via our contact form.
  • Automated Information: IP addresses, browser type, and device information gathered automatically via Google Analytics for security and traffic monitoring purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Any information collected is used strictly for:

  • Responding to inquiries or messages sent through our contact page.
  • Monitoring website performance and preventing security threats (e.g., DDoS attacks).
  • Improving the accessibility and user experience of the memorial archive.

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3. Cookies & Analytics

This website uses cookies to enhance user experience. We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with the site (e.g., which pages are visited most). These cookies collect anonymous data and do not identify you personally.

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

4. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to external sites (e.g., YouTube for video evidence, Facebook for community updates). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

5. Data Security

NexGuard Web Solutions implements industry-standard security measures to protect this website. We use SSL encryption (HTTPS) to ensure that any data transmitted between your browser and our servers is secure.
