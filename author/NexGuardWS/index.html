About the Author

NexGuard Web Solutions serves as the editorial and technical lead for RememberingHadi.online. While the content of this website is derived from public records, police investigations (DMP), and historical archives, the curation and security of this data are managed by our specialized team.

Founded in the wake of the political shifts of 2025, our mission goes beyond standard web development. We believe that digital history is fragile. Without robust security and dedicated maintenance, the stories of martyrs like Sharif Osman Hadi risk being erased by cyberattacks or technical neglect.

Our Editorial Standards

As the maintainers of this archive, we adhere to strict standards of verification:

  • Fact-Checking: All claims regarding the assassination timeline are cross-referenced with official police reports and reputable news outlets (e.g., The Daily Star, Prothom Alo).
  • Multimedia Integrity: CCTV footage and images are presented without manipulation to preserve evidentiary value.
  • Security First: We ensure that visitor data remains private and that the site remains accessible despite potential external threats.

Note: NexGuard Web Solutions operates voluntarily for this cause. We are not a political party, but a technical collective demanding justice through digital preservation.
